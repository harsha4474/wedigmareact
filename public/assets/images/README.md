# Image Assets Guide

## Directory Structure

- `/assets/images/` - General images, hero images, banners
- `/assets/logos/` - Client and partner company logos
- `/assets/team/` - Team member photos
- `/assets/tech-stack/` - Technology and tool logos
- `/assets/screenshots/` - Product screenshots and case study images

## Image Specifications

- **Logos**: PNG or SVG, transparent background, max 200px height
- **Team Photos**: JPG, 400x400px, professional headshots
- **Screenshots**: PNG, 1200x800px or 16:9 ratio
- **Hero Images**: JPG, 1920x1080px or higher

## Temporary Placeholders

Until real images are added, the site uses:
- https://via.placeholder.com for image placeholders
- https://ui-avatars.com for team member avatars
- SVG icons for technology logos
